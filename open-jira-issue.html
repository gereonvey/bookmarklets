Jira Issue Bookmarklet

Here is how you open a Jira issue easily

Create the bookmarklet in your browser

  1. Enter the base URL of your Jira Instance (including "https://"):
  2. Bookmark the following link, so that it appears in your bookmarks bar: Open Jira Issue
Now, each time you encounter a Jira issue which is not linked, you can still easily get to it:
  1. Mark the issue ID on a webpage
  2. Click on the bookmarklet and it will take you to the Jira issue

As an example, you could mark the issue TEAM-1000 in this text to go to the Jira page of the issue.
